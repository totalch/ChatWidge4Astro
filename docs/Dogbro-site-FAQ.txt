## 💡 General & Corporate

### What does Dogbro LLC do?

Our core services span four pillars: AI-Driven Content Generation, Automated Data Scraping & Insights (DaaS), AI-Powered Technical Support Bots, and Rapid AI UI/UX Prototyping.

**Yes.** We offer full **White-Label licensing** across our entire workflow suite. You can package our data scraping pipelines, content generation tools, or support bots, apply your own branding, and deploy them directly to your clients or integrate them into your existing B2B software ecosystem.

## ✍️ 1. AI-Driven Content Generation

### Are there any copyright issues with the text generated by your platform?

**None at all.** Unlike platforms that scrape copyrighted materials, we train and fine-tune our open-source models using clean, copyright-free data (such as Wikipedia, Common Crawl, and public domain literature). The content generated belongs entirely to you, with zero intellectual property risk.

### How does your AI ensure the content sounds authentic and stays on-brand?

We don't use generic, one-size-fits-all prompts. We fine-tune models to mirror your specific brand voice, industry vocabulary, and formatting guidelines. This ensures your automated blog posts, social media updates, and SEO copy read as if they were written by an in-house expert.

## 📊 2. Data Scraping & Insights (DaaS)

### How is Dogbro different from standard data scraping tools on the market?

Standard tools only give you messy, raw spreadsheets that take hours to clean. Dogbro provides **Data-as-a-Service (DaaS)**. We handle the extraction, clean the data, feed it through our AI analysis engine, and deliver a clean, interactive visual dashboard filled with actual strategic recommendations.

### Is automated data scraping legal and compliant?

Yes, when done ethically. We strictly scrape **publicly accessible data** (such as e-commerce prices, public financial records, competitor product catalogs, and social trends) while fully respecting web scraping best practices, robots.txt files, and data privacy regulations. We do not extract private or personally identifiable information (PII).

## 🤖 3. AI Technical Support & FAQ Bots

### How do your support bots learn about our specific products?

We construct a secure, private knowledge base using your internal documentation, product wikis, API guides, and historical customer support tickets. The AI agent refers *only* to this verified dataset, ensuring accurate answers while preventing it from hallucinating or making up information.

### How does your "pay-per-query" pricing work?

Instead of forcing you into a rigid enterprise license, we offer transparent, usage-based billing. You are billed based on the volume of interactions (e.g., blocks of 1,000 queries resolved). If your customer volume drops, your bill drops; if your business grows, your support infrastructure scales seamlessly without needing to hire more staff.

### What do we actually receive when we order an AI prototype?

You will receive a live, fully interactive, and completely editable **Figma or FigJam link**. The layout, components, and user flows are generated by our AI layout engines based on your product concept, allowing your team to immediately jump in, modify, or present the design to stakeholders.

### Can we use these prototypes to pitch to investors?

**Absolutely.** That is exactly what they are built for. Instead of spending thousands of dollars and weeks of development time with a design agency just to show a proof-of-concept, our AI creates high-impact, visual wireframes in a fraction of the time, letting you validate ideas or secure early funding fast.
